Guacamole

Ingredients to make an Guacamole

  • Ingredients for How to Make Easy Mexican Guacamole
  • 40 gr of leofilized avocado brand Nëofruits®
  • 50 gr. onion
  • 1 medium tomato
  • 2 tablespoons fresh coriander leaves
  • 2 medium juicy lemons
  • Optional (if you like spicy, it's essential): 1 serrano pepper (if you can't find it, you can use canned jalapeños, chilli or cayenne)
  • Salt (to taste at home)
  • To accompany: Totopos or corn triangles (nachos) even tortillas or bread

how to make Guacamole

  1. We peel the onion and wash the tomato. We split them into cubes
  2. We take our Nëofruits® brand freeze-dried avocado and pour it into a bowl with ¾ glass of water to let it hydrate for 15 minutes.
  3. So we will avoid leaving and knowing if they are in excellent condition, since our products are in good condition.
  4. finally crush the ingredients

Avocado Sauce

Ingredients to make an Avocado Sauce

  • 40 gr of leofilized avocado brand Nëofruits®
  • 25 gr. onion (about 1/4 medium onion)
  • 20gr of fresh coriander
  • 1 medium green tomato or half of a large one
  • ¼ cup of milk
  • 2 medium juicy lemons
  • Optional (if you like spicy, it is essential): 1 serrano or jalapeño pepper)
  • Salt to taste

how to make Avocado Sauce

  1. Peel the onion and chop it into pieces
